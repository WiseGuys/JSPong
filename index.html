Johann Sebastian Pong

Play the classic Pong game that you know and love, but redesigned and more awesome.

Controls: Use WASD or the arrow keys to control your paddle and press SPACE to serve

Description: In a world where music was taken away, fight back with your Pong skills to get the music back!

Created by: Bill Derouin, Michael Bandemer, Indran Kamalanathan, Dillon Smith and Fletcher Groseclos
